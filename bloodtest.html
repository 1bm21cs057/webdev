Our Services

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

A complete blood count (CBC) is a blood test used to evaluate your overall health and detect a variety of disorders, including anemia, infection, and leukemia. The CBC can help diagnose and monitor a range of medical conditions and provide important information about your health, including red blood cell counts, white blood cell counts, and platelet counts.

Liver Function Test (LFT)

A liver function test (LFT) is a blood test used to check how well the liver is working. The test measures the levels of certain chemicals in the blood that are normally produced by the liver. Abnormal results can indicate liver damage, disease, or dysfunction.

Renal Function Test (RFT)

A renal function test (RFT) is a blood test used to assess how well the kidneys are functioning. The test measures the levels of certain chemicals in the blood that are normally filtered out by the kidneys, such as creatinine and urea. Abnormal results can indicate kidney damage, disease, or dysfunction.
